Welcome to Shuvakamana English Boarding School (SEBS)

Our School offers an inquiry based, child centered and academically rigorous curriculum that addresses the intellectual, social and emotional needs of each child .We have a secure environment where students’ talents are identified, respected and carefully nurtured by all. All students are consistently encouraged in a positive manner by our staff to try their best in all areas of the curriculum. Through this atmosphere of warmth and encouragement, students’ self esteem and their autonomy as learners are consistently developed. As our classes are taught in English from Nursery onwards, students develop a very high level of proficiency in the language over the course of their schooling, a fact that has often been noted and celebrated by our occasional visits by foreign teachers.

At a glance, we currently have 600 above students enrolled from Nursery to Class 10. In coming years, we will also extend the curriculum up to Class 12. We provide a secure learning environment in which the value of a good education is communicated to our students both explicitly and implicitly through the dedication and highly honed skills of our staff who work respectfully and compassionately with students at all times. We believe that attention to the task at hand is a pivotal skill to be mastered, therefore students are expected to behave respectfully towards their teachers and their fellow students at all times and to value the importance of the education they receive here. As students grow in understanding of the value of their studies, they too learn to support and nurture the efforts of their classmates and also of students junior and senior to them. This leads to a very positive atmosphere within the school where each student feels valued. Our students are proud of their school, their opportunities to learn, their community, the natural environment, their country and their world.

We believe in “Working Together, Learning Together and Acting Together”. Students are frequently involved in curriculum related field trips and opportunities to learn from experts within the community. A warm, caring, supportive and successful learning community has always evolved at Shuvakamana English Boarding School. All children have the opportunity to excel and to develop skills and knowledge that will set them on the path to fulfillment and success as future adult citizens of our country. We hope that your child will grow in knowledge, wisdom and com-passion during their time of learning and maturation at our school.

Mission, Vision and Objectives

Vision

Our vision is to prepare our students for a rapidly changing world by developing English Language skills of the 21st Century, generating critical thinking skills, a global perspective, and a respect for core values of honesty, loyalty, perseverance, and compassion.

Mission

Our Mission is to provide high quality education and a safe learning environment with a welcoming and friendly atmosphere by maintaining an inclusive environment which acknowledges and respects children from diverse family and cultural backgrounds.
